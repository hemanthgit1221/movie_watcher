# MOOVAA Privacy Policy (draft)

**Effective date:** TBD before public launch

## Data we collect

- Device identifier and platform (for alerts and push notifications)
- FCM registration token (to send booking alerts)
- Alert preferences: movies, cities, theatres, time slots
- Optional watchlist and notification preferences stored on our servers

## How we use data

- Operate ticket-availability alerts
- Send push notifications when bookings open
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse

## Data deletion

Users may call `DELETE /api/v1/devices/me` (authenticated) to remove subscriptions, notification history, watchlist, and deactivate the device record.
